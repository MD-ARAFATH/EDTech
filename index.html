what is a semantic tag? They describe the role of elements (e.g., header, article, nav, footer). They improve accessibility for users and screen readers. They boost SEO by helping search engines understand page structure.
what is a box model? Content: The actual text, images, or other content within the element. Padding: Clear space around the content, inside the border. Border: A visible line surrounding the padding and content. Margin: Clear space outside the border, separating elements.
